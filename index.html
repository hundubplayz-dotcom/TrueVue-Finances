About This Project

The Story Behind the Data

TrueVue is a virtual company I built and managed during my senior year of high school through Virtual Enterprise International (VEI), a program where students create and run simulated businesses in a real-world competitive marketplace alongside thousands of other student firms worldwide.

Our product — TrueVue RoadLock — was a smart dash-cam and driver-safety software suite. Over seven months (October 2025 through April 2026), my team secured a B2B contract, managed trade show sales across Southern California, and built an e-commerce channel, generating over $1.26 million in total revenue.

Now a college freshman studying business and computer science, I built this dashboard to bridge those two worlds — applying real financial data to a frontend development project as a learning exercise in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and data visualization.

All financial figures shown are authentic records from our accounting master document. Months labeled "Projected" (Apr 2026) reflect approved budget plans, not actuals.
